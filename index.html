02 · File specs

Preparing your file

Formats
PDF (preferred) or PNG. If you're unsure whether your file will work, send it anyway — we'll check it before printing and follow up if anything needs adjusting.
Resolution
300 DPI or higher for crisp results.
Bleed
If your layout includes bleed and cut lines, we'll follow them. If not, we'll confirm trim with you before cutting.
Delivery
Attach files under 20 MB directly to your order email. For larger files, paste a share link (Google Drive, Dropbox, Proton Drive, etc.) in the form below — just make sure the link is set to “anyone with the link can view.”

03 · Place an order

Order form

This form composes an order email in your own mail app — your details go straight to us and nowhere else. Prefer to skip the form? Email orders@pringleprints.ca directly with the same details.

“Open order email” launches your mail app with everything filled in — attach your file there if it's under 20 MB, then hit send. “Copy order summary” copies the same text so you can paste it into any email or message.

04 · What happens next

After you order

We'll confirm your order and file by email, flag anything that needs fixing before it goes to print, and let you know the timeline. Payment is by e-transfer once your order is confirmed. Printing and hand-cutting a batch takes real time — we'll always give you an honest estimate rather than a fast one.

We currently serve the Ottawa–Gatineau region for pickup and local delivery. Canada-wide shipping is on the way — if you're elsewhere in Canada, get in touch anyway and we'll see what we can work out.
